General Introduction

Baby Massage/Yoga is a great way for you Mums to strengthen the bond between you and your baby while also promoting good health for both you and your little one.

Baby Massage

Baby Massage is suitable for (precrawlers) babies between 6 and 8 weeks.


Baby Massage classes will teach you techniques which will help:

  • Sleep patterns
  • Will give relief from trapped wind and constipation
  • Circulation
  • Stimulates lymphatics

Baby massage has been practices for years and the techniques you will learn will benefit your babies health greatly.

Baby Yoga

Baby Yoga is recommended for babies of at least 10 weeks of age.


Our yoga classes are designed to enhance development, both physical and behavioural using:

  • Gentle stretches
  • Rthymic movement
  • Sensory play

In our classes, we incorporate both songs and rhymes.

This is more physically engaging and for this reason is recommended for babies of at least 10 weeks of age.
