What is Baby Óga?

Baby Óga was set up to help strengthen the bond between mother and child in a fun way.

This one on one time spent massaging or exercising with your baby is a great way to enhance the bond between you and your little one!

There are many incredible benefits for both mother and child.

Some of these benefits include:
  • Improved sleep patterns
  • Relief of gastric symptoms (colic and constipation)
  • Enhanced bonding
  • Stimulated lymphatics

What do we have to offer?

Baby Yoga

Yoga based exercises designed to enhance baby development through gentle stretches, rhythmic movement and sensory play.

Learn more about yoga

Baby Massage

Between 6 weeks to 8 months (precrawlers) you are invited with your baby to attend a 5 week course in the art of touching and massage.
